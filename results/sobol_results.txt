DIAGNOSTIC FINDING (this is a real result, not a numerical artifact):
mu0H_max, frequency and fluid_mdot show ~0 sensitivity because the
current amr_cycle.py model structure makes COP_electrical algebraically
independent of Qc: COP_electrical = 1 / [(Th/Tc-1)/eta_2nd_law(eps) +
parasitic_fraction], since both W_mag and W_parasitic scale linearly
with Qc and cancel. Field/frequency/flow rate DO change how much cooling
you get (Qc, via dTad) but NOT, in this model, how efficiently you get it.
Real AMR systems don't have this decoupling -- eddy-current losses scale
with frequency^2, viscous dissipation with mdot^2, and magnet support/
motor sizing with field -- so eta_2nd_law and parasitic_fraction should
be state-dependent functions, not constants. This is flagged as a
required Phase 3 model upgrade in ROADMAP.md, not silently patched here.
